# Online Store Annual Sales Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the annual performance of an online retail store using sales and order data. The objective is to identify key business trends, customer behavior patterns, and high-performing sales channels to support data-driven decision-making.

The analysis was performed using spreadsheet tools and dashboard visualizations.

## Business Objectives

The following questions were addressed in this project:

1. What are the top 5 states by total sales?
2. How are orders distributed across different age groups?
3. What is the annual order status distribution?
4. How do sales and orders vary by month?
5. Which gender contributes the highest sales percentage?
6. Which sales channels generate the most orders?

## Dataset

The dataset contains:

* Customer demographics (age, gender, location)
* Order details
* Sales amount
* Order status
* Sales channels
* Monthly transaction data

## Tools Used

* Microsoft Excel
* Pivot Tables
* Charts and Dashboard Visualizations


## Key Insights

* Peak sales occurred between **March and June**, with Maharashtra contributing the highest revenue during this period.
* **Female customers generated a larger share of total sales** compared to male customers.
* The **top-performing sales channels** were:
  * Amazon
  * Myntra
  * Flipkart
* The majority of orders came from the **young and middle-aged customer segments**.
* Most orders were successfully delivered, indicating strong fulfillment performance.

## Business Conclusions

1. **Seasonal Strategy:**
   Sales peak in the March–June period. Marketing campaigns and inventory planning should be increased before this window.

2. **Target Customer Segment:**
   Female customers form a major revenue segment. Personalized campaigns and product lines should be designed for this audience.

3. **Channel Optimization:**
   Amazon, Myntra, and Flipkart are the most effective channels. More promotions, ad spending, and partnerships should be focused there.

4. **Regional Focus:**
   Maharashtra shows strong sales performance. Similar strategies can be applied to other high-potential states.

5. **Operational Efficiency:**
   High delivery success rates indicate a stable logistics system, which can support scaling sales volume.
